BEST MOVIES ACCORDING TO HARSH

My top 3 movies

1.Cars

Cars is a 2006 American animated sports comedy film
produced by Pixar Animation Studios and released by Walt Disney Pictures.
The film follows Lightning McQueen, a race car who ends up in a small town and learns valuable life lessons.

2.Cars2

Cars 2 is a 2011 American animated spy comedy film produced by Pixar Animation Studios and released by Walt Disney Pictures.
The film is a sequel to the 2006 film Cars and features the characters Lightning McQueen and Mater as they travel around the world to compete in the World Grand Prix.

3.Cars3

Cars 3 is a 2017 American animated sports comedy-drama film produced by Pixar Animation Studios and released by Walt Disney Pictures.
The film is the third installment in the Cars franchise and follows Lightning McQueen as he faces a new generation of racers.
